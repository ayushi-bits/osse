The Black Box

Innovation Think Tank, Siemens Healthcare

Many potential safety hazards exist in medical products’ transportation and storage processes. If a product breaks in a logistics process, it is hard to guarantee the consumers’ safety, or even worse, may do harm to the company’s reputation and make it difficult to divide the responsibility of the incidents. So, it is extremely necessary to start supervising from the first step logistics process. I designed a black box for detecting causes for system and components damage/failure and monitoring status during their product lifecycle using integrated sensors and a data analysis system.
